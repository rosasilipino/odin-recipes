Meatloaf

Enjoy a moist and delicious delight full of flavor.

Get rid of those soft bananas. Friends and family will be begging for more. Best served warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (175 degrees celcius).
  2. Lightly grease a 9x5- inch loaf pan.
  3. In large bowl combine flour, baking soda, salt.
  4. In seperate bowl cream together butter and brown sugar.
  5. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas once butter and brown sugar are creamed.
  6. Combine seperate bowl into larger bowl, mix into batter.
  7. Pour batter into loaf pan.
  8. Bake in over for 60 - 65 minutes. Checking periodically.
  9. Once done, let cool for 10 minutes.
