Tuna Alfredo

Creamy, savory alfredo tuna afredo. Quick and easy to make for you or the family.

Can easy add/substitute with steamed vegetables such as peas or brocoli.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of fettuccine pasta
  • 1 jar alfredo sauce
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp drained capers
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 5 ounces drained can tuna

Steps

  1. Bring pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook until liking.
  2. Drain pasta and set aside.
  3. Stir in alfredo sauce, lemon juice, capers, lemon zest pepper into a small saucepan.
  4. Add tuna, cook over medium heat for 5 mins. Serve over pasta.
