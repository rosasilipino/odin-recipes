Meatloaf

Quick and easy meatloaf recipe. Simply can be one of the family's favorite meals.

Great to pair with a side of vegetables, mashed potatoes or creamy mac and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (175 degrees celcius).
  2. In large bowl, mix together beef, egg, onion, milk, bread crumbs and salt/pepper.
  3. Lightly grease a 9x5- inch loaf pan before placing mixture into pan.
  4. In seperate bowl mix brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over meatloaf.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
